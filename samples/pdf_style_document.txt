What this kind of system really is

A deterministic document question-answering system retrieves evidence from uploaded files.
It does not generate prose answers with an LLM.
Users ask questions and receive citation-backed evidence cards.

Existing architectures

The most common pre-generative architecture is the enterprise search stack.
A second architecture is the classic QA pipeline.
A third architecture is the ontology and knowledge-graph stack.
A fourth architecture is the traceability and citation graph.

Architecture comparison

Teams often compare enterprise search stacks with classic QA pipelines when planning evidence systems.

Open-source building blocks

For ingestion, the most useful open-source building blocks include PDF parsers and text extractors.
Indexing uses inverted lexical indexes with BM25 ranking.
Storage uses SQLite for local persistence.

Common capabilities

Lineage is one of the most important concepts in traceability workflows.
Lineage means history and traceability of something across a workflow.
Examples include which geometry was used and which dataset was used.

Ways to make systems feel intelligent without AI

Structured extractive answers can list architecture families from section text.
Section-level retrieval improves list-style questions.
Deterministic query interpretation routes questions to the right retrieval path.

Requirements for evidence quality

Every answer must cite source line ranges.
Evidence cards must show matched terms and confidence levels.
No invented content is allowed outside retrieved snippets.

Limitations

PDF extraction quality depends on source formatting.
Heading detection may miss unusual layouts.
Lexical retrieval does not understand synonyms without explicit rules.

Evaluation checklist

Reviewers should verify section retrieval, structured answers, and citation coverage.

Future improvements

Better table extraction would help requirement documents.
Multi-document comparison remains limited in the first version.
Enterprise connectors are documented as future scope.

Conclusion

TraceDoc Engine prioritizes traceability and deterministic behavior over generative fluency.
